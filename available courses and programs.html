ZCC Industrial Training College

National Certificate Courses

These are courses that one may be able to complete after 1 year or 2 years of learning at the institution and requires one to have at least 5 O Level subjects including Mathematics and English with some courses not requiring Mathematics

NC COURSES INCLUDE

    MANAGEMENT COURSES

    A minimum of 5 O Level Subjects including English, Mathematics is not a requirement

    Course duration is One Year
  • Micro Enterprice Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Purchase and Supply Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Transport and Logisticts Management

    • Agricultural NC COURSES

    A minimum of five O Level subjects including Mathematics and English

    Course duration is 2 years where one year is spent at College and another one on industrial Attachment
  • Horticulture
  • Animal Production

    • A minimum of 5 O level subjects including Mathematics and English

    Course Duration is 1 year and studied over 2 semesters
  • Information and Communication Technology
  • Accountancy

    • Building COURSES

    A minimum of five O Level subjects including Mathematics and English

    Course duration is 3 years comprising of 2 years in College and 1 year on attachment leading to a Skilled Worker Class Two Certificate and the National Certificate
  • Building Technology

    • On successful completion, a graduate is awarded a NATIONAL CERTIFICATE which qualifies one to enroll for a Diploma in the area

National Foundation Certificates

These are courses that one may be able to complete within a period of 1 year of learning at the institution and requires a applicant to have at least 1 O Level pass with students choosing at least 2 courses from the list below

NFC COURSES INCLUDE

  • Bookkeeping
  • Word Processing
  • Business Calculations
  • Computer Programming
  • Computer Operations and Packages
  • ICT Fundamentals
  • Animal Production
  • Crop Production
  • Ornamental Horticulture
  • Brick and Block Laying
  • Drain Laying and Rural Sanitation
  • Plastering

    • On successful completion a graduate is awarded a NATIONAL FOUNDATION CERTIFICATE which is a professional qualification recognised in and outside Zimbabwe
